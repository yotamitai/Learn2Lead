Instructions

There are two players in this game: “worker” and “fetcher”.
The goal of the worker is to go to the correct numbered station (red frame).
The goal of the fetcher is to go to the corresponding colored toolbox (labeled T), obtain the tool, and then go to the worker’s station.

A given scenario will look similar to the image on the right:

Remember: The fetcher does not know the worker's goal and will need to infer it based on the worker's actions.

You control the worker: the circular blue agent, labelled “W”.

Your main objective should be to act in such a way that the fetcher joins you at the goal station with a MINIMAL number of steps.

The game controls:

Tab - Pause/Unpause
Up - Move up
Left - Move left
Down - Move down
Right - Move right
Space - Work (press when reaching the goal station)
Enter - Wait (don't move for one turn)

Good luck!
